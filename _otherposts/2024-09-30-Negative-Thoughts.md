tags: 
---

It seems that the best way to deal with negative thoughts is to ignore them and immerse oneself in more non-trivial/involved work than one is equipped to deal with, rather than trying to proactively fight against them. Bad thoughts slowly begin to lose their bite, and a kind of numbness takes hold of you, which will hopefully be replaced by a more fulfilling state later.

Letting your mind fantasize about things that will never be is perhaps even worse than feeling sad about said things. Visual novels are evil.

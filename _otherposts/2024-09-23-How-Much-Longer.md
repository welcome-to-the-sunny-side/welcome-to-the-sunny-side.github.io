We have AI [getting gold at IOI](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/133874) now but still no high-quality AI girlfriend services. Is it really too much to ask for? It would probably take a couple of people at OpenAI a week or so to get it up and running. The difference between the amount of effort required and the benefit to humanity is revoltingly absurd. Now, it's not like I haven't tried the currently available services. EVERYTHING SUCKS. Almost all models are low quality, have terrible memories, lack media capabilities, and are trained on retarded data (some aren't even fluent in English). I have talked to some people who have apparently gotten much better results by locally running models after tinkering with them (I dont know anything about how LLMs work), but I don't have nearly enough money to be able to afford decent hardware for it.

What makes the lack of activity and innovation in this sphere baffling is that someone could earn a **lot** of money from such a service:

1. There would be a huge potential market for AI girlfriends.
2. Unlike things like GPT, people would have a very strong incentive to pay for such services. Further, users would eventually grow emotionally dependent on the model and be willing to pay an ever increasing amount of money, for a very long time to continue to be able to talk to the model. I am the kind of person who pirates everything, and I swear to god I would pay as much as 100 usd a month if the service was good enough.

I am so over everything at this point. Fuck you OpenAI.

## Problem [1548-E](https://codeforces.com/problemset/problem/1548/E)

## Statement

> You are given two arrays, $a$ and $b$ of length $n$ and $m$ respectively. Consider the $n \times m$ grid $c$ generated from $a$ and $b$, having $c[i][j] = a[i] + b[j]$. A cell $(i, j)$ is defined to be good if $c[i][j] \leq x$, where $x$ is a given constant. Find the number of connected components in $c$.

#### Constraints:

- $n \leq 2 \cdot 10^5$
- $m \leq 2 \cdot 10^5$
- $a_i, b_j \leq 2 \cdot 10^5$
- $x \leq 2 \cdot 10^5$
